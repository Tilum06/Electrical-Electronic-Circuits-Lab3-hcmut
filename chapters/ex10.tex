\subsection{Theoretical calculation}

\textbf{\textit{Notes}}

\textit{Explanations, formulas, and equations are expected rather than only results.}\\

Apply KVL for the left loop, we have:
\[-V_1 + I_E \cdot R_1 + V_{BE} =0\]
\[\Longleftrightarrow I_E = \frac{V_1 - V_{BE}}{R_1} = \frac{4~\text{V} - 0.7~\text{V}}{1.2~\text{k}\Omega} = 2.75~\text{mA}\]

From that, we can calculate:
\begin{itemize}
    \item $I_B = \frac{I_E}{\beta + 1} = \frac{2.75~\text{mA}}{100 + 1} \approx 27.2~\mu\text{A}$
    \item $I_C = \beta \cdot I_B = 100 \cdot 27.2~\mu\text{A} = 2.72~\text{mA}$
\end{itemize}

Apply KVL for the right loop, we have:
\[V_2 - I_C \cdot R_2 - V_C = 0\]
\[\Longleftrightarrow V_C = V_2 - I_C \cdot R_2 = 10~\text{V} - 2.72~\text{mA} \cdot 2.4~\text{k}\Omega = 3.472~\text{V}\]
\[\Longleftrightarrow V_{CE} = V_C - V_E = 3.472~\text{V} - (-0.7~\text{V}) = 4.172~\text{V}\]
