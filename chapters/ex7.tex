\subsection{Theoretical calculation}
The transistor Q2 is OFF because $V_E < V_B$.

Then, we can calculate the values for transistor Q1 as follows:

Applying KVL, we have (assuming $\beta$ = 100):
\begin{align*}
    V_A &= I_B * R_B + V_{BE} + I_E * R_E \\
    I_E &= (1 + \beta) * I_B = 101I_B\\
    V_A &= I_B * R_B + V_{BE} + 101I_B * R_E \\
    I_B &= \frac{V_A - V_{BE}}{R_B + 101R_E} = \frac{5V - 0.7V}{10k\Omega + 101 * 1k\Omega} = 38.7 \mu A \\
    I_C &= \beta * I_B = 100 * 38.7 \mu A = 3.87 mA \\
    I_E &= I_C + I_B = 3.87 mA + 38.7 \mu A = 3.91 mA \\
\end{align*}
Calculating voltages at all nodes:
\begin{align*}
    V_B &= V_{BE} + I_E * R_E = 0.7V + 3.91 mA * 1k\Omega = 4.61 V \\
    V_E &= I_E * R_E = 3.91 mA * 1k\Omega = 3.91 V \\
\end{align*}
